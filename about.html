about me...

I work with the Smarter Energy Group at IBM Research, Bangalore. Currently taking my bachelors in Information and Communication Technology from DA-IICT, I am often in pursuit of substance in the fields of techonology and art.

On the work front, I like to focus my attention to aesthetics, while I am designing or prototyping ideas. I code, sketch, paint and draw, sometimes with pencils and sometimes with pixels. I believe that a good design can solve some of the most complex problems in the world.

Cognitive sciences almost always draw my attention. Creative applications of technology, math, human computer interaction (esp. natural user interfaces), information design, softwares, cloud computing and philosophy are some of the areas that I wish to explore.

I am 21.

about the site

I wanted my portfolio to be largely typographic because good typography, in my view, is something that expresses the content and the meaning while maintaining the aesthetics of the website. It eliminates the need for unnecessary chrome.

The site is called seaflection because of its link with the meaning of my name. The neologism becomes relevant for me :).
